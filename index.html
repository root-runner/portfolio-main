<about>

I'm a veterinary medicine student, junior frontend developer and recreational pilot.

Mid-way through 2022 I began a 12-month period of clinical rotations as part of my final year of Vet. When I'm not on placement or studying, I keep myself busy by developing code, flying and working (I also enjoy relaxing, especially with a cold beer).

I have been programming part-time for approximately 3 years. My passion for it began in year 9, when I learnt about terminal on the school computers - that was a whole lot of fun... Since then, it has always been something that I've continued doing as a hobby, but now that I am close to the end of my studies I want to take it to the next level.

I'm looking for my first step into the software industry. A career in frontend development is something that I would like to pursue alongside veterinary medicine. Indeed, I may even combine the two - I believe there are huge gaps in the vet industry where technology has fallen behind, and I absolutely LOVE innovation.

</about>
